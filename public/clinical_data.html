Safety Profile

Understanding the safety considerations of Mounjaro (tirzepatide) therapy.

Common Side Effects

  • Nausea (12-18%)
  • Diarrhea (13-17%)
  • Decreased appetite (6-12%)
  • Vomiting (5-9%)

Important Safety Information

  • Not for use in patients with a personal or family history of medullary thyroid carcinoma
  • Not for use in patients with Multiple Endocrine Neoplasia syndrome type 2
  • Monitor for pancreatitis

Monitoring

  • Monitor blood glucose prior to starting and during treatment
  • Monitor for signs of pancreatitis
  • Monitor for diabetic retinopathy in patients with a history of diabetic retinopathy
